About

Matias Marti

Hello there! I develop websites and web applications. The technologies I use are: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6), React, Node, Express, and PostgreSQL. I have also made projects with jQuery, Sass, Bootstrap v3 & v4, and MongoDB. Almost all my code is available on Github. Currently, I am taking online courses taught by senior full-stack developers on Frontend Masters and Udemy.

The application I am the most proud of is called parkingMapp. It makes sure people avoid getting parking tickets by coloring the map. More information can be found further down.

Apart from development, I have an interest in 3d printing. I let my creativity flow when designing in 3d and making physical items using my printer.
